Plant Care

Expert Care Guide

wb_sunny

Light Requirements

Understanding your plant's light needs is essential. Most tropical plants thrive in bright, indirect light. Avoid direct sunlight which can burn leaves.

water_drop

Watering Schedule

Water when the top 2 inches of soil feels dry. Overwatering is the most common cause of plant death. Ensure proper drainage in all pots.

air

Humidity & Temperature

Tropical plants love humidity between 60-80%. Use pebble trays or humidifiers for optimal growth. Maintain temperatures above 60°F (15°C).
